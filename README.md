lsd
===

You can play with this stupid tool just with : 

> lsd Hello world

or a better one 

> lsd \`man man\`

or 

> lsd \`cat lsd.c\`

or

> lsd \`fortune\`

or

> lsd *


or whatever you want.


Help me to colorize the world (randomly :D).
